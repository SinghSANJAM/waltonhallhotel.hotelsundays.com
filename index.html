Sunday Warwickshire Walton Hall Hotel & Spa

A luxurious 4-star hotel set in a 16th-century Grade II listed estate, Walton Hall offers 197 rooms, a modern spa, fine dining, and 22 event spaces across 65 acres of tranquil Warwickshire countryside, just 10 minutes from the M40.

Explore Rooms
Rooms & Suites

Historic Charm & Modern Luxury

Choose from 197 beautifully appointed rooms, including 37 luxury rooms in our historic 16th-century Hall. Enjoy modern amenities, plush beds, high-speed Wi-Fi, and serene countryside views.

Spa & Leisure

Step into Tranquility

Nestled in Warwickshire’s countryside, our spa features an indoor pool, sauna, steam room, fitness studio, and a range of luxurious face and body treatments for ultimate relaxation.

Getting Here

Travel to Walton Hall Hotel

Distance from M40, Junction 13 (Northbound) – 10 miles / 15 minute drive

From M40 Northbound

Exit M40 at Junction 13, follow signs to Wellesbourne.

Turn right after the Watermill and follow the single lane for 1.5 miles.

From M40 Southbound (J15)

Exit M40 at Junction 15, take A429 to Wellesbourne.

Turn right after the Watermill and follow the single lane for 1.5 miles.

Nearest train station – Warwick – 7 miles / 15 minute drive

Birmingham Airport – 25 miles / 30 minute drive
