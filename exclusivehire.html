Exclusive Hire at Walton Hall

Make your special event truly extraordinary by reserving the entire Walton Hall estate. Our exclusive hire option gives you and your guests complete privacy and unparalleled access to our historic manor house, beautifully manicured grounds, and luxurious amenities.

Perfect for corporate retreats, grand celebrations, or intimate family gatherings, the estate includes elegant event spaces, beautifully appointed rooms, and a dedicated team to cater to your every need. Enjoy exclusive access to our spa, swimming pool, and pristine gardens for a truly memorable experience.
