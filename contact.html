Getting Here

Travel to Walton Hall Hotel & Spa

Exit M40 at Junction 13, follow signs to Wellesbourne.

Turn right after the Watermill.

Follow the single lane for 1.5 miles.

The hotel will be on your right.

Exit M40 at Junction 15, take A429 to Wellesbourne.

Turn right after the Watermill.

Follow the single lane for 1.5 miles.

The hotel will be on your right.

Nearest train station – Warwick Parkway – 6 miles / 15 minutes drive.

Bus services available to Wellesbourne, followed by a short taxi ride.
