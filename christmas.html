Festive Celebrations

A Magical Christmas

Sunday Warwickshire Walton Hall Hotel & Spa offers the perfect setting for a memorable Christmas and New Year. Nestled in 65 acres of picturesque countryside, our 16th-century Hall provides a magical backdrop for your festive celebrations. Whether you're seeking a relaxing short break, mouth-watering festive menus with all the trimmings, or sparkling nights of fun with friends or colleagues, we have a wonderful selection of ideas to make your Christmas truly special.

Festive Packages

Your Perfect Celebration

All That Sparkles Party Nights

The perfect festive party night for you, your friends, and colleagues! The build-up to Christmas is just as magical as the main event. Sit back, relax, and enjoy a prosecco or two as you party the night away at Walton’s All That Sparkles Party Night.

Booking Details: Dress to impress, savor a mouth-watering Christmas meal, and dance to great music, creating memories that last beyond the festive season. Available for private parties of 50 or more. Contact us to book.

Festive Getaway

Why not make your Christmas party a festive getaway? Arrive in the afternoon, take your time to get ready, party the night away, and enjoy a leisurely breakfast before you leave.

Booking Details: Limited rooms are available and can only be booked once deposits are paid. Upgraded bedrooms are available at a supplement for an extra touch of luxury.

Magical Winter Weddings

We’ll transform your winter wedding dreams into reality with a fairy-tale setting and a sprinkling of festive magic. Our historic 16th-century Hall and dedicated team ensure a romantic and unforgettable celebration.

Booking Details: Contact our team to plan your winter wedding. Limited dates available.
